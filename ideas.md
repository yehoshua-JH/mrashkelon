# Mr. Ashkelon — Design Brainstorm

## Design Philosophy Chosen: **Mediterranean Heritage Luxury**

**Design Movement:** Coastal Mediterranean Modernism — drawing from the warm tones of Israeli stone architecture, the deep blues of the Mediterranean Sea, and the golden light of the Negev desert edge.

**Core Principles:**
1. Warm authority — Navy and gold convey trust and premium quality without coldness
2. Typographic contrast — Serif display headings (Georgia-class) paired with clean sans-serif body text
3. Asymmetric content blocks — Alternating image-left/text-right layouts break monotony
4. Generous whitespace — Cream/off-white backgrounds let content breathe

**Color Philosophy:**
- Deep Navy `#1a2d4f` (hsl 220 46% 20%) — Primary brand, headers, nav
- Warm Gold `#c9a84c` (hsl 40 55% 55%) — Accents, CTAs, dividers
- Cream `#faf8f4` — Page backgrounds, warm not clinical
- Soft Stone `#f0ece4` — Card backgrounds
- Dark Slate `#2c3e50` — Body text

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial — hero sections use full-bleed imagery with left-aligned text overlays; content sections alternate between left-image/right-text and right-image/left-text; property cards use a magazine-style grid.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Gold horizontal rule dividers beneath section headings
2. Navy top navigation bar with gold "Get in Touch" CTA button
3. Subtle warm paper texture on cream sections

**Typography System:**
- Display/H1: Georgia serif, bold, deep navy
- H2/H3: Georgia serif, regular, with gold underline accent
- Body: system-ui sans-serif, slate, 1.7 line-height
- Nav: system-ui sans-serif, medium weight, uppercase tracking

**Animation:** Subtle fade-in-up on scroll for section content; smooth hover transitions on cards and nav links; no jarring motion.
